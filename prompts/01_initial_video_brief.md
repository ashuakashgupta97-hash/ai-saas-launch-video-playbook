# Prompt 01: Initial Video Brief

Use this prompt when you want to convert a business use case into a launch-video direction.

```text
I want to create a 75-90 second SaaS/product launch video for an email order intake platform with AI-assisted review.

The video should explain:
1. Current pain points
2. Platform features
3. Positive business impact

Pain points:
- Teams receive orders through email.
- A person manually scans the email chain.
- They open attachments and identify the customer.
- They identify the distributor or partner.
- They check confirmation.
- They read product lines and quantities.
- They separate relevant products from irrelevant products.
- They map product names to product codes.
- They prepare an Excel file for a downstream system.

Platform features:
- Connects to an order mailbox.
- Lists email threads in one queue.
- Reads email body and attachments.
- Suggests customer and partner matches with confidence scores.
- Maps products and quantities.
- Lets the user review and correct results.
- Downloads a final Excel output.

Please create:
- a crisp voiceover script
- a scene-by-scene video storyline
- suggested on-screen text
- where screenshots or screen recordings should appear
- KPI ideas for the closing scene

Keep the tone professional, enterprise-ready, and focused on workflow impact.
Do not use real company, customer, vendor, email, or product names.
```
