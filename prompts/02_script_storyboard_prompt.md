# Prompt 02: Script And Storyboard Refinement

Use this when the first script is directionally right, but needs sharper structure and timing.

```text
Refine the video script into a 90-second structure.

The flow should be:
0-6 sec: AI-assisted product opener
6-34 sec: current manual burden
34-40 sec: transition into three-step workflow
40-50 sec: Step 1, select email from live queue
50-60 sec: Step 2, verify customer and partner
60-70 sec: Step 2 continued, verify products and quantities
70-76 sec: Step 3, download final Excel
76-90 sec: KPI impact and closing line

For each scene, provide:
- voiceover
- on-screen text
- visual direction
- screenshot or animation requirement
- transition idea

Keep the script concise enough for a natural voiceover.
Avoid generic AI buzzwords unless they are tied to a specific workflow benefit.
```
