# Sample Input Brief

## Product

Email order intake platform with AI-assisted review.

## Audience

Operations leaders, billing teams, transformation stakeholders, and product sponsors.

## Current Process

The team receives order requests through email. Each request may contain an email body, PDF attachment, Excel attachment, or image. A user manually opens the thread, checks the source document, identifies the customer, identifies the distributor, reads products and quantities, maps product names to codes, and prepares an output file.

## New Platform

The platform connects to an order mailbox, lists order threads in one queue, parses documents, suggests customer and distributor matches, maps products and quantities, and lets the user download an Excel file after review.

## Desired Story

Move from inbox-heavy manual processing to a guided review workflow.

## Required Scenes

- manual email burden
- product queue
- document review
- customer and distributor verification
- product and quantity mapping
- Excel export
- KPI impact

## Constraints

- no real company names
- no real customer names
- no real email addresses
- no confidential screenshots
- 90 seconds maximum
