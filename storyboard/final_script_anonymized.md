# Final Script - Anonymized Example

AI driven Email Order Intake helps teams move from inbox-heavy order processing to guided, review-ready workflows.

Today, billing teams receive customer and distributor orders over email.

For every order, a person scans the email chain, opens every attachment, identifies the customer, enters the correct customer code, identifies the distributor, enters the correct distributor code, and checks distributor confirmation.

Then they read every product line, separate relevant products from other company products, search product codes, map each product to the applicable contract, enter quantities, and prepare Excel for the downstream system.

The new platform changes this into a guided AI-assisted workflow with just three simple steps.

Step one: select an email from the live order queue. Every email order thread appears in one place, with customer, distributor, source, region, and product count visible upfront.

Step two begins once the source document opens on the left. On the right, the system suggests the customer and distributor with confidence scores and master-linked alternatives.

Then verify products and quantities. The system reads line items, maps products to product codes, keeps evidence visible, and lets the user correct mappings or quantities quickly.

Step three: download the final Excel and post it into the downstream system, making the process really quick.

The result is fewer clicks, fewer product-code errors, faster order preparation, shorter fulfillment time, and a clear audit trail from email to Excel.

This is AI driven Email Order Intake.
